VJP Packaging Industries Ltd.






VJP manufactures folding cartons, printed blanks and outers, litho-lamination, plastic cartons, blister packs andshelf-ready packaging.

 VJP INDUS. has also ventured into the flexible packaging industry, with capability to produce printedcork-tipping paper, laminates, sleeves and wrap-around labels.

In 2019, VJP INDUS. achieved a turnover of greater than Rs. 800 Crore. VJP INDUS. is proud to have over 1,600 employees.

VJP Packaging is promoted by the Kanoria family, which has varied business interests such as jute, tea, textiles, pharmaceuticals and chemicals.

In 1990, the Kanoria family ventured into folding cartons manufacturing, with a unit in Silvassa.
